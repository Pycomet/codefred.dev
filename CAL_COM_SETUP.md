# Cal.com Setup Guide for Consultation Bookings (FREE PLAN)

This guide will help you configure your Cal.com FREE account with 4 event types for your consulting services.

## Prerequisites

1. **Cal.com Account**: If you don't have one, sign up at [cal.com](https://cal.com)
2. **Username**: Ensure your Cal.com username is set (e.g., `codefred`)
3. **Calendar**: Connect your Google Calendar for automatic scheduling

## Free Plan Workflow

**Important**: Cal.com free plan doesn't include payment collection. Here's how it works:

1. ‚úÖ Client books a call (FREE via Cal.com)
2. üìß You receive booking confirmation email
3. üí∞ **You send invoice separately** (via email, PayPal, bank transfer, etc.)
4. ü§ù Payment confirmed before call (or trust-based for established clients)
5. üìû Call happens as scheduled

**Payment Methods** (handled outside Cal.com):
- Bank transfer / Zelle
- PayPal invoice
- Wise / TransferWise
- Trust-based (invoice after call for long-term clients)

```markdown
- Teams with urgent deadlines
- Projects that are stuck or behind schedule
- Critical bug fixes or performance issues
- New feature implementation with tight timelines

**Investment**: $2,500 for 1 week
**Next Available**: Check calendar for upcoming sprint slots
```

### Advanced Settings
- **Buffer Time Before**: 30 minutes
- **Buffer Time After**: 30 minutes
- **Minimum Notice**: 5 days (need prep time)
- **Date Range**: Show availability 2-8 weeks out
- **Booking Frequency**: Maximum 2 per month

2. **Project Description** (Required, Long text) - What needs to be built/fixed?
3. **Current Tech Stack** (Required, Long text)
4. **Sprint Goal** (Required, Long text) - What must be complete by end of week?

## Troubleshooting

**Q: How do I handle payments on free plan?**
- Bookings are free via Cal.com
- Send PayPal invoice or bank details via email after booking
- Confirm payment before call
- Consider upgrading to paid plan if you want automated payment collection

**Q: Bookings not showing on calendar?**
- Check Google Calendar integration
- Verify calendar permissions
- Refresh Cal.com calendar sync

**Q: Wrong timezone?**
- Update your Cal.com profile timezone to WAT (UTC+1)
- Verify client sees times in their local timezone

**Q: Want to upgrade for payment features?**
- Cal.com paid plans start at $12/month
- Includes Stripe integration, team features, and more
- Upgrade when booking volume justifies the cost
