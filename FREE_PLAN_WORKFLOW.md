## Payment Methods Setup

### Option 1: PayPal (Recommended - Easy for International)
1. Create PayPal.me link: `paypal.me/yourname`
2. Share link for quick payments
3. Or send invoices via PayPal dashboard

**Pros**: Easy, international, buyer protection
**Cons**: ~3% fee

### Option 2: Bank Transfer (Nigeria)
1. Share your bank details template
2. Good for local Nigerian clients
3. Instant with most Nigerian banks

**Pros**: No fees, instant
**Cons**: Nigeria-only, requires trust

### Option 3: Wise (TransferWise) - Best for International
1. Create Wise business account
2. Get account details for USD, EUR, GBP, NGN
3. Low fees, fast transfers

**Pros**: Multi-currency, low fees (~1%)
**Cons**: Requires setup

### Option 4: Cryptocurrency (Optional)
- USDT/USDC for international clients
- Good for clients who prefer crypto
- Use Binance or Coinbase

**Pros**: Fast, low fees
**Cons**: Requires crypto knowledge

## FAQ

**Q: What if client doesn't pay but shows up to call?**
- Politely remind them before call
- You can proceed (build trust) or reschedule
- Your choice based on client relationship

**Q: Partial payment for expensive services?**
- Implementation Sprint: 50% upfront ($1,250), 50% after
- Fractional CTO: First month upfront, then monthly

**Q: Client disputes or refund?**
- Have clear refund policy in confirmation email
- For Cal.com bookings: Refund through same payment method
- Generally: No refunds <24hrs before call

**Q: Invoice for accounting?**
- Create invoice in PayPal, Wave, or Google Docs
- Include: Invoice #, Date, Service, Amount, Payment method
- Send copy with payment confirmation
